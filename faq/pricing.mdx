## Pricing Plans

Unsiloed AI offers flexible pricing to suit different use cases:

- **Free Trial** - Test the platform with your documents
- **Pay-as-you-go** - Pay only for what you use
- **Enterprise** - Custom plans with dedicated support and volume discounts

[Sign up on Unsiloed AI](https://cal.com/aman-mishra-p0ry57/15min) to discuss pricing options.

### How is usage calculated?

Usage is based on:
- Number of pages processed
- API calls made
- Document size and complexity

Monitor your usage in real-time through your dashboard.

### Payment Methods

We accept credit cards, ACH transfers (enterprise), and wire transfers (annual contracts).

## Billing

- **Monthly plans** - Billed at the beginning of each month
- **Pay-as-you-go** - Billed based on actual usage monthly
- **Annual plans** - Billed upfront with a discount

All invoices are available in your dashboard under "Billing."

## Enterprise

Enterprise plans include dedicated support, custom SLAs, volume discounts, on-premise deployment options, and personalized training.

[Contact sales](mailto:hello@unsiloed-ai.com) or [sign up](https://cal.com/aman-mishra-p0ry57/15min) for a custom quote and demo.
